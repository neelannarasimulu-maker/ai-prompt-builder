# BMA/Open

Block Markets Africa / Open brand: professional financial infrastructure and wallet enablement brand for client qualification, wallet business cases, payments, customer activation and deal readiness.

Palette: navy #0B2F44, deep teal #006C70, BMA green #67B345, Open green #56C947, soft mint #EAF5F3, cool grey #EEF3F3, charcoal #243238, white #FFFFFF.

Style: clean, credible, executive and document-led. Use white or soft mint document canvases, navy section headers, teal rules, green action accents and clear form tables. Avoid hype, decorative clutter, fake metrics and generic fintech dashboards.

Typography: professional sans-serif for headings and readable serif or sans-serif for body where appropriate. Keep tables highly legible.
