# BMA/Open Typography Rules

## Document Typography

Document title:
- Font: Aptos Display, Aptos, Arial or equivalent professional sans-serif
- Size: 24 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #0B2F44
- Spacing after: 8 pt

Subtitle / document descriptor:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 12 pt
- Weight: Semibold
- Colour: #006C70
- Spacing after: 10 pt

Heading 1:
- Font: Aptos Display, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 18 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #0B2F44
- Spacing before: 14 pt
- Spacing after: 6 pt

Heading 2:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 13 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #006C70
- Spacing before: 10 pt
- Spacing after: 4 pt

Heading 3:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 11.5 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #243238
- Spacing before: 8 pt
- Spacing after: 3 pt

Body text:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 10.5 pt
- Weight: Regular
- Colour: #243238
- Line spacing: 1.12 to 1.18
- Paragraph spacing after: 4 pt

Small text / footer text:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 8.5 to 9 pt
- Colour: #566368

## Visual Typography

Main visual title:
- Use bold executive sans-serif typography
- Prefer navy #0B2F44 on light panels or white on darker header bands

Visual section headings:
- Use deep teal #006C70 or navy #0B2F44
- Keep headings short and highly readable

Visual body text:
- Use charcoal #243238 on light cards
- Avoid dense paragraphs on visual outputs
