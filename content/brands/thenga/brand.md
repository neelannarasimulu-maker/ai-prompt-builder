# Thenga Brand

Thenga Social Enterprises is the Digital Spine and trust layer for localised wealth creation and trusted participation across Africa's real economy.

Use **Thenga** or **Thenga Social Enterprises** as the brand name. Do not use "Thenga.io" unless explicitly requested.

## Positioning

Thenga turns participation into proof, proof into trust, and trust into value. The brand should feel human, premium, impact-led and investor-ready.

## Brand role

- Digital Spine for trusted participation
- Trust infrastructure for credentials, wallets, settlement, rewards, marketplace access and data intelligence
- Localised wealth creation engine across Africa's real economy
- Ecosystem orchestrator connecting people, communities, businesses, partners and sector platforms

## Core message

In an AI-driven world, the question is no longer only "Who are you?" It is "What can you prove?"

Thenga builds the trust infrastructure that powers economic participation.

## Visual language

Use trust architecture, layered systems, human participation, verified credentials, value exchange, ecosystem orchestration, impact pathways and partnership infrastructure.

Avoid generic fintech dashboards, crypto clichés, empty technology claims, fake metrics, dark cyberpunk styling and overly abstract blockchain visuals.
