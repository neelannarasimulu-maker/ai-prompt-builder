# Thenga Logo Rules

Use the extracted Thenga Social Enterprises logo asset provided in the brand assets folder.

## Primary usage

For dark, navy, teal or image-based backgrounds, use:
`content/brands/thenga/assets/thenga-logo-transparent-dark.png`

For white, soft sand, pale mint or light document-style backgrounds, use:
`content/brands/thenga/assets/thenga-logo-transparent-light.png`

For small marks, icons, badges or footers, use:
`content/brands/thenga/assets/thenga-icon-transparent.png`

## Handling rules

- Do not recreate the logo.
- Do not recolour the icon.
- Do not stretch, distort, crop or rotate the logo.
- Keep clear space around the logo.
- Do not place the white wordmark version on a light background.
- Do not place the navy wordmark version on a dark background.
- If the renderer cannot place the logo image, reserve clean header space and use the text `THENGA SOCIAL ENTERPRISES`.

## Prompt-builder note

The PNG logo assets should be referenced by path in prompts. Do not inline large base64 logo data into visual or document prompts.
