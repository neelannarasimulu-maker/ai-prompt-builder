# Thenga Typography Rules

## Document Typography

Document title:
- Font: Aptos Display, Montserrat, Arial or equivalent executive sans-serif
- Size: 24 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #102A43
- Spacing after: 8 pt

Subtitle / document descriptor:
- Font: Aptos, Montserrat, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 12 pt
- Weight: Semibold
- Colour: #008B8B
- Spacing after: 10 pt

Heading 1:
- Font: Aptos Display, Montserrat, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 18 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #102A43
- Spacing before: 14 pt
- Spacing after: 6 pt

Heading 2:
- Font: Aptos, Montserrat, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 13 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #008B8B
- Spacing before: 10 pt
- Spacing after: 4 pt

Heading 3:
- Font: Aptos, Montserrat, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 11.5 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #1E2A32
- Spacing before: 8 pt
- Spacing after: 3 pt

Body text:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 10.5 pt
- Weight: Regular
- Colour: #1E2A32
- Line spacing: 1.12 to 1.18
- Paragraph spacing after: 4 pt

Small text / footer text:
- Font: Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 8.5 to 9 pt
- Colour: #59646A

## Visual Typography

Main visual title:
- Use bold executive sans-serif typography
- Prefer white on deep navy panels or Deep Ubuntu Navy #102A43 on light panels

Visual section headings:
- Use Trust Teal #008B8B, Prosperity Gold #D6A11E or Deep Ubuntu Navy #102A43
- Keep headings short and highly readable

Visual body text:
- Use Charcoal Ink #1E2A32 on light cards
- Use white or soft sand text on dark panels
- Avoid dense paragraphs on visual outputs
