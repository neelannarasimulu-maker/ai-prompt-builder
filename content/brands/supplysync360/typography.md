# SupplySync360 Typography Rules

## Document Typography

Document title:
- Font: Montserrat Bold, Aptos Display, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 24 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #006C70
- Spacing after: 8 pt

Subtitle / document descriptor:
- Font: Open Sans, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 12 pt
- Weight: Semibold
- Colour: #008B8B
- Spacing after: 10 pt

Heading 1:
- Font: Montserrat Bold, Aptos Display, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 18 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #006C70
- Spacing before: 14 pt
- Spacing after: 6 pt

Heading 2:
- Font: Open Sans Semibold, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 13 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #008B8B
- Spacing before: 10 pt
- Spacing after: 4 pt

Heading 3:
- Font: Open Sans Semibold, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 11.5 pt
- Weight: Bold
- Colour: #2F4F4F
- Spacing before: 8 pt
- Spacing after: 3 pt

Body text:
- Font: Open Sans, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 10.5 pt
- Weight: Regular
- Colour: #2F4F4F
- Line spacing: 1.12 to 1.18
- Paragraph spacing after: 4 pt

Small text / footer text:
- Font: Open Sans, Aptos, Arial or equivalent
- Size: 8.5 to 9 pt
- Colour: #5D6D6D

## Visual Typography

Main visual title:
- Use Montserrat Bold or equivalent
- Prefer white on medium-depth teal backgrounds or deep teal on light panels

Visual section headings:
- Use deep teal #006C70 or vivid teal #008B8B

Visual body text:
- Use charcoal #2F4F4F on mist or white panels
- Use signal yellow #FFD700 only for restrained action emphasis
