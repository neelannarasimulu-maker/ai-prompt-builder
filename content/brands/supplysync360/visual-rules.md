# SupplySync360 Visual Rules

Use premium executive operational-technology styling.
Use vibrant teal and deep-teal gradients with visible brand depth, not washed-out white and not black-heavy.
Use lighter content panels for readability.
Balanced backgrounds should read as brighter executive canvases with saturated colour around edges, header bands, corner geometry, connector paths and accent lines.
Use visible data-flow cues and one controlled signal-yellow action accent where useful.
Avoid fake dashboards, generic icons, clutter, beige/grey blandness, muddy graphite backgrounds and overly dark control-room backgrounds.
