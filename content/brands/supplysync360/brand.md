# SupplySync360

SupplySync360: premium executive operational technology for supply chain visibility, supplier coordination, inventory control and AI-assisted execution.

Palette: teal #008B8B, deep teal #006C70, signal yellow #FFD700, charcoal #2F4F4F, graphite #1F2F2F, mist #F4FAFA, white #FFFFFF, soft grey #E8F1F1.

Style: premium executive technology design with vibrant teal/deep-teal gradients, bright readable content panels, crisp data-flow cues and one controlled signal-yellow action accent where useful. Light, balanced and dark backgrounds should all carry brand colour, gradients and energy; they differ by brightness and contrast, not by becoming bland. Balanced backgrounds should use saturated teal bands, luminous route lines, shaped header/footer depth and bright content panels without becoming dark-dominant. Avoid washed-out white slides, flat pale canvases, beige/grey blandness, muddy graphite, full black backgrounds, fake dashboards, generic icons and clutter.

Typography: Montserrat Bold or equivalent for headings. Open Sans or equivalent for body text.
