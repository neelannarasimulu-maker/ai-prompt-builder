# RainFin Visual Rules

Use premium advisory-forum styling with soft brand-aware depth.
Prefer balanced navy, teal and rain-blue gradients that are not too dark and not too light.
Use white or soft mist readable content cards.
Use AVBOB green and Ninety One gold only as restrained status or partner accents.
Avoid black-heavy backgrounds, overly graphite styling, fake dashboards, neon effects and washed-out white canvases.
