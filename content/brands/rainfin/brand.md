# RainFin

RainFin: financial technology and programme-operations brand for regulated wallet, savings, KYC, reporting and partnership execution.

Palette: navy #173A59, rain blue #1EA7E1, sky blue #36B3E7, deep teal #063543, white #FFFFFF, soft mist #F5FAFC.

Style: premium advisory-forum design with vibrant but controlled navy, teal and rain-blue gradients. Use light-to-medium background depth, luminous flow texture and white or mist readable content cards. Light, balanced and dark backgrounds should all retain brand colour and gradients; balanced backgrounds should stay brighter than dark while using saturated rain-blue/navy bands, connector paths and accent glows. Avoid backgrounds that are too dark, black-heavy, overly graphite, flat white, washed out, beige/grey bland, neon or dashboard-like.

Typography: clean executive sans-serif. Use strong headings, clear hierarchy, readable cards and generous spacing.
