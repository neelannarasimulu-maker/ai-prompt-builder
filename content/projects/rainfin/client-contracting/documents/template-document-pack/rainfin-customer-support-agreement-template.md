This Customer Support Agreement is entered into as a separate annexure to the **RainFin Master Services Agreement** entered into between the Parties, the “MSA”, and applies to the support services provided by RainFin in respect of the services described in the applicable **RainFin Transaction Document**. Unless the context requires otherwise, capitalised terms used in this Customer Support Agreement have the meaning given to them in the MSA or the applicable Transaction Document.

## 1. Scope of Support Services

1.1 The Service Provider offers two types of support services:

1.1.1 24/7 production platform / fix-on-fail support; and

1.1.2 operational, day-to-day support services.

1.2 The support included in this Customer Support Agreement is the following:

1.2.1 support and maintenance of the RainFin platform and related Open network services, which is fix-on-fail support;

1.2.2 problem resolution, being the satisfactory resolution of production system problems associated with the RainFin platform or Open network services within RainFin’s direct control; and

1.2.3 change management, being the management of changes to production, development and testing environments of the RainFin platform or Open network services to resolve problems and add new functionality to meet agreed business needs.

1.3 Any other support services are scoped and quoted for separately according to the Client’s needs and requests.

1.4 This Customer Support Agreement does not include the availability or down time of any third-party service provider and is limited to the Service Provider’s systems over which the Service Provider has direct control.

## 2. Services

2.1 **Core Support Hours:** 08:00 to 17:00 GMT+2, Monday to Friday, excluding South African public holidays.

2.2 **Technical Assistance:** Addressing issues related to the functionality and performance of the supported services.

2.3 **Incident Management:** Resolving incidents reported by the Client within the response and resolution times set out in this Customer Support Agreement.

2.4 **System Updates:** Providing updates, patches or upgrades to support service performance, security and compliance, where applicable.

2.5 **Client Training:** Offering training resources and documentation to assist the Client in using the supported services effectively, where applicable.

2.6 **Knowledge Base Access:** Access to a repository of frequently asked questions, troubleshooting guides and best practices, where available.

2.7 **Included Services:**

2.7.1 assistance with account setup and management;

2.7.2 technical support for wallet-related issues, including login, transactions and access; and

2.7.3 guidance on service usage.

2.8 **Excluded Services:**

2.8.1 troubleshooting issues unrelated to the supported services;

2.8.2 issues caused by misuse, unauthorised modifications, or third-party software or hardware;

2.8.3 support for third-party software or hardware;

2.8.4 on-site support, unless explicitly agreed in writing; and

2.8.5 regulatory or legal advice.

## 3. Contact Methods for Services

3.1 Support will be provided through the following channels:

3.1.1 **Email Support:** support@rainfin.com

3.1.2 **Help Desk Portal:** Access to an online portal for ticket submissions and tracking, where made available by RainFin.

3.1.3 **Phone Support:** Direct phone support during business hours at +27 10 003 2331, or such other number as RainFin may notify to the Client in writing.

## 4. Support Levels and Response Times

4.1 **Platform Availability Times:** The Service Provider will target at least 99.0% platform availability during the following times:

4.1.1 availability measures will not include planned downtime as agreed between the Parties;

4.1.2 Monday to Sunday, including South African public holidays;

4.1.3 24 hours a day; and

4.1.4 365 days a year.

4.2 **Fault Severity Levels:** The Service Provider will respond to support requests based on the following priority levels:

| Fault level | Impact | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | Extensive / Widespread | A critical system failure which has a material impact on the system’s ability to provide end-to-end client service. |
| Major | Significant / Limited | Where the system remains active but users are severely impacted. |
| Low | Minimal / None | Day-to-day operational queries not meeting the definition of Critical or Major. |

4.3 **Fault Response and Resolution Times:** The Service Provider will make reasonable efforts to resolve issues within the following timelines and update the Client in cases where resolution may take longer.

4.3.1 Resolution times are only applicable to the core support hours set out in this Customer Support Agreement.

4.3.2 The Service Provider does not guarantee uninterrupted access to support services due to factors beyond its control, including internet outages, third-party failures or force majeure events.

4.3.3 Resolution times may vary depending on the complexity of the issue and the level of support requested.

| Fault level | Initial response | Resolution time |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Critical | Inbound on call | Within 4 hours |
| Major | 1 hour | Within 1 business day |
| Low | 1 business day | Within 5 business days |

## 5. Client Responsibilities

5.1 The Client shall report incidents promptly and provide accurate and detailed information about the issue, including screenshots or error messages, if applicable.

5.2 The Client shall provide necessary access to systems and personnel to facilitate troubleshooting and resolution.

5.3 The Client shall maintain secure access to service credentials and avoid sharing sensitive information through insecure channels.

5.4 The Client shall utilise the RainFin platform, Open network and supported services in accordance with the Service Provider’s terms of use, the MSA, the applicable Transaction Document and Applicable Laws.

## 6. Term and Termination

6.1 **Term:** This Customer Support Agreement commences on the Effective Date and remains in effect until terminated by either Party with 30 days’ written notice or such longer period as may be agreed between the Parties.

6.2 **Termination for Cause:** Either Party may terminate this Customer Support Agreement if the other Party materially breaches its obligations under this Customer Support Agreement and fails to remedy the breach within 14 days of written notice.

6.3 Termination of this Customer Support Agreement shall not, by itself, terminate the MSA or any Transaction Document unless expressly agreed in writing or required by the MSA.

## 7. Confidentiality and Data Privacy

7.1 The Service Provider agrees to keep Client information confidential and comply with applicable data protection regulations.

7.2 The Client agrees to notify the Service Provider immediately of any suspected data breach or unauthorised access related to its account or use of the supported services.

7.3 Data privacy, data processing and related information security obligations are dealt with in the MSA and the separate **RainFin Data Processing Agreement**, where applicable.

## 8. Governing Law

8.1 This Customer Support Agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

## 9. Entire Agreement

9.1 This Customer Support Agreement, read together with the MSA and the applicable Transaction Document, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties concerning the support services described herein.

9.2 This Customer Support Agreement supersedes all prior agreements or understandings between the Parties relating specifically to the support services described herein.

9.3 Any amendments to this Customer Support Agreement must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
