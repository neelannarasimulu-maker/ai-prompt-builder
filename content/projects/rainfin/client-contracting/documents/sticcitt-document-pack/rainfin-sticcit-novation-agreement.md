# Novation, Release and Replacement Services Agreement

**Agreement transition from MPay to RainFin for Sticcit replacement and new services**

## Parties

This **Novation, Release and Replacement Services Agreement** is entered into between:

**MPAYDIGITAL (PTY) LTD**
Registration number: 2020/666507/07
Address: WeWork, 80 Strand Street, City Centre, Cape Town, 8001
the “Outgoing Service Provider” or “MPay”
(the “Service Provider”)

and

**RAINFIN (PTY) LTD**
Registration number: 2008/029213/07
Address: First Floor Suite 5a, Waterstone Office Park, Cnr Main Road & R44, Somerset West, Western Cape, 7130
the “Incoming Service Provider” or “RainFin”

and

**STICITT (PTY) LTD**
Registration number: 2014/095879/07
Address: 10 Niblick Way, Somerset West, Cape Town, 7130
the “Client” or “Sticcit”

Together, the “Parties”.

## 1. Background

1.1 MPay and Sticcit are parties to an agreement titled **Master Services Agreement**, dated **04 February 2025**, together with any schedules, addenda, annexures and amendments thereto, the “Original Agreement”.

1.2 The Parties record that the services previously provided by MPay to Sticcit under the Original Agreement are to be discontinued, transitioned and replaced by services to be provided by RainFin directly to Sticcit.

1.3 This Agreement records the transition from MPay to RainFin, the release of MPay in relation to future services, and the basis on which RainFin will contract directly with Sticcit for replacement and new services.

## 2. Effective Date

2.1 This Agreement takes effect on **the date of the last signature of this agreement**, the “Effective Date”.

## 3. Replacement Services and Follow-on RainFin Agreements

3.1 From the Effective Date, MPay shall cease providing services to Sticcit under the Original Agreement, except for any transition assistance expressly agreed in writing.

3.2 From the Effective Date, RainFin will contract directly with Sticcit for the replacement and new services.

3.3 The replacement and new services to be provided by RainFin shall be governed by separate agreements and documents to be entered into between RainFin and Sticcit, including:

3.3.1 **RainFin Master Services Agreement**;
3.3.2 **RainFin Data Sharing Agreement**;
3.3.3 **RainFin Customer Support Agreement**; and
3.3.4 **RainFin Transaction Document**.

3.4 This Agreement does not itself set out the full commercial, technical, data, service level, support or operational terms for RainFin’s services. Those terms will be set out in the RainFin agreements and documents listed in clause 3.3.

## 4. Novation, Release and Substitution

4.1 To the extent required to give effect to the transition, Sticcit consents to RainFin replacing MPay as the service provider for the replacement services from the Effective Date.

4.2 From the Effective Date, Sticcit shall look to RainFin, and not MPay, for the provision of the replacement and new services agreed under the RainFin agreements and documents listed in clause 3.3.

4.3 RainFin accepts responsibility only for the services it agrees to provide to Sticcit from the Effective Date under the RainFin agreements and documents listed in clause 3.3.

4.4 RainFin does not assume liability for MPay’s acts, omissions, breaches, unpaid amounts, historic service issues or obligations arising before the Effective Date, unless RainFin expressly agrees otherwise in writing.

4.5 MPay is released from any obligation to provide future services to Sticcit under the Original Agreement from the Effective Date.

## 5. Accrued Rights and Historic Matters

5.1 Nothing in this Agreement releases any Party from accrued rights, obligations, claims, fees, reconciliations or liabilities arising before the Effective Date, unless expressly agreed in writing.

5.2 Any fees, charges, refunds, reconciliations, disputes or liabilities arising before the Effective Date shall remain between the relevant Parties under the Original Agreement.

5.3 Any fees, charges or obligations arising from RainFin’s services after the Effective Date shall be governed by the RainFin agreements and documents listed in clause 3.3.

## 6. Transition Cooperation

6.1 The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to ensure an orderly transition from MPay to RainFin.

6.2 The Parties shall provide such operational, technical, support, reconciliation and access information as may reasonably be required to support the transition, subject to applicable confidentiality and data protection obligations.

## 7. Authority

7.1 Each Party warrants that it has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement.

7.2 Each person signing this Agreement warrants that they are duly authorised to sign on behalf of the Party they represent.

## 8. Governing Law

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.

## 9. Counterparts and Electronic Signature

9.1 This Agreement may be signed in counterparts.

9.2 This Agreement may be signed electronically, and any electronic signature shall be valid and binding to the extent permitted by applicable law.
