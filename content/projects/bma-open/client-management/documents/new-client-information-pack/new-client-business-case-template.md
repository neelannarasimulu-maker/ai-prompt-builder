# BLOCK MARKETS AFRICA / OPEN

# New Client Business Case Template

**Lightweight decision pack for assessing whether to proceed with a prospective wallet Client**

Purpose: capture the minimum information needed to evaluate opportunity scale, wallet adoption potential, balance and flow economics, strategic fit and readiness for a next-stage deal review.

This is not a commercial proposal, compliance due diligence pack, contract pack, implementation plan or customer success scorecard.

## How this template should be used

The prospective Client completes this document with enough evidence for BMA/Open to decide whether the opportunity warrants further investment of time, solution design and contracting effort.

The core test is wallet potential: eligible customer base, reachable customers, expected active wallets, average balances, inflows, payment flows and activation channels.

Items such as detailed compliance due diligence, pricing, SLAs, implementation KPIs, customer success metrics and formal contracting are intentionally moved to later engagement stages.

# 10. Proposed engagement document journey

Use the right document at the right stage. This avoids asking the Client for a heavy pack before BMA/Open has qualified the deal.

| Stage | Document | Owner | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Opportunity introduction | Opportunity teaser / introductory note | BMA/Open or Client | Frame the idea, use case and initial interest. |
| 2. Business case stage | New Client Business Case Template | Client completes, BMA/Open reviews | Assess wallet scale, Customer reach, expected active wallets, balances, flows and strategic fit. |
| 3. Internal qualification | BMA/Open Deal Qualification Memo | BMA/Open internal | Decide whether to proceed, hold or decline. Capture assumptions and review gaps. |
| 4. Discovery and solution fit | Discovery Pack and Solution Fit Note | Joint | Confirm product scope, user journeys, data, integrations, launch path and effort. |
| 5. Due diligence | Compliance, KYB/KYC, AML, POPIA and risk pack | Joint, led by BMA/Open compliance/legal | Assess regulatory, operational and data risk once the opportunity is qualified. |
| 6. Commercial proposal | Commercial Proposal / Term Sheet | BMA/Open | Set commercial model, commercials, responsibilities and commercial assumptions. |
| 7. Contracting | Master Agreement, SOW, service schedules and data terms | Legal and commercial teams | Formalise contracting party, obligations, services, fees, data, SLA and liability terms. |
| 8. Implementation readiness | Implementation Plan and Launch Readiness Checklist | Joint | Plan technical, operational, support, training, communications and launch activities. |
| 9. Customer success | Customer Success Plan and Performance Pack | Joint after contract / launch | Define KPIs, adoption cadence, support status, reporting, invoice management and ongoing governance. |
