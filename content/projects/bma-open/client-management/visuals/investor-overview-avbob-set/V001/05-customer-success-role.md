## Intent

Position Neelan’s Customer Success role as the programme discipline that turns platform capability into adoption, measurable value, target delivery and investor-visible proof.

## Visible Text

Title: Customer Success: From Platform To Measurable Adoption
Body: Customer Success in this programme is not passive account management. It is programme orchestration across adoption strategy, KPI definitions, value targets, volume targets, AUM targets, delivery cadence and executive reporting.
Body: The role connects business intent, technical delivery, operational readiness and member adoption into a measurable customer-success system.

Section: KPI Definitions and Tracking
Metric: Registrations: number of members registered for the wallet
Metric: Activation rate: registered members completing first meaningful wallet action
Metric: Active users: members using wallet, token, payment, reward or savings capability
Metric: Transaction volume: top-ups, cash-outs, token sends, token receipts and retailer payments
Metric: Redemption activity: voucher issue, usage and direct point-of-sale redemption
Metric: Savings adoption: members using savings goals or growth-earning balances
Metric: AUM: customer balances participating in the money-market-backed value proposition
Metric: Support quality: support volumes, resolution time and issue closure

Section: Phase Delivery Approach
Item: Phase 1: controlled readiness, pilot onboarding and operating cadence
Item: Phase 2: staff and member activation at scale
Item: Phase 3: public launch, rewards expansion and retailer payment growth
Item: Phase 4: optimisation, repeat usage, AUM growth and programme expansion

Section: Target Management
Action: Define registration and activation targets by launch phase
Action: Define value and volume targets for wallet usage
Action: Define AUM targets for growth-earning balances
Action: Define retailer payment and voucher redemption targets
Action: Track weekly movement against agreed programme KPIs
Action: Convert KPI evidence into expansion and investor proof

## Image Brief

Create a customer-success control-room visual for a VC investor. Show KPI definitions, phase delivery, value targets, volume targets and AUM targets as a governed operating rhythm. Use clear metric rows, phase gates and action ownership. Avoid generic CRM styling. Make Neelan’s role feel like an enterprise programme operating office.
