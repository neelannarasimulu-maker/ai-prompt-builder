## Visible Text

Title: The Opportunity Behind the Programme
Body: Large member-based organisations need more than a wallet. They need a governed participation layer that connects members, value, benefits, rewards, payments, savings and service journeys.
Body: Many enterprise programmes struggle because product, compliance, technology, onboarding, payments, support, retailer redemption and adoption operations sit in disconnected workstreams.
Body: The AVBOB programme gives Open/RainFin a practical platform to prove that these pieces can be coordinated into one repeatable operating model.

Section: Programme Problem
Item: Member onboarding must be simple, trusted and operationally reliable.
Item: KYC and identity flows must support enterprise-scale adoption.
Item: Wallet top-ups, cash-outs, token transfers and retailer payments must work without customer transaction fees.
Item: Rewards and vouchers must be redeemable directly at point of sale.
Item: Savings value must be visible, credible and easy to understand.
Item: Enterprise stakeholders need reporting, governance, launch cadence and issue ownership.

Section: Commercial Opportunity
Body: If the model works for AVBOB, the same operating pattern can support insurers, employers, member associations, unions, benefit-led ecosystems and affinity communities.
Body: The opportunity is not a single wallet deployment. It is a repeatable enterprise customer-success and embedded-finance operating model.
