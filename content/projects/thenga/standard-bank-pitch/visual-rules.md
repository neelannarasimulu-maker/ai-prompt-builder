# Standard Bank Pitch Visual Rules

Use the Thenga brand system and keep the output investor-ready, strategic and partnership-oriented.

## Visual guidance

- Position Thenga as trusted participation infrastructure for Africa's real economy.
- Keep the Digital Spine, trust infrastructure, participation, credentials, wallets, value exchange, data and impact language aligned to the source MD file.
- Use the official Thenga logo from the brand assets folder.
- Use premium readable backgrounds with deep ubuntu navy, trust teal, prosperity gold, earth green, warm clay and soft sand.
- For slide backgrounds, keep the overall balance lighter than dark: the centre or main reading zones should be brighter and more open, with richer brand colours used for edge depth, header treatment, corner geometry, section dividers and accents.
- Avoid overly dark, black-heavy or neon styling.
- Do not add unsupported claims, fake metrics or invented partnership statements.

## Output discipline

Use `## Visible Text` as the only on-image text source for visual outputs.
Use `## Body Content` as the source of truth for document outputs only.
