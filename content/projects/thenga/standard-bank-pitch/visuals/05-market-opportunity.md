## Intent

Demonstrate the scale of the opportunity and show how Thenga accesses multiple participation economies through a shared Digital Spine.

## Visible Text

### Africa's Real Economy Is Vast, Fragmented and Under-Served

Millions of creators, traders, suppliers, farmers, healthcare workers, entrepreneurs and communities participate in economic activity every day. Yet many remain disconnected from trusted infrastructure, market access and financial participation.

The participation challenges facing Africa are not unique to Africa. Similar opportunities exist across emerging markets where communities, creators, healthcare participants, suppliers and small enterprises remain disconnected from trusted infrastructure and economic opportunity.

Thenga creates practical pathways that connect participation with opportunity, services, institutions and capital.

### Key Participation Economies

Lifestyle & Entertainment
Connecting creators, audiences, sponsors and commercial opportunities.

Healthcare & Community Services
Enabling accessible, measurable and community-led healthcare participation.

Agri-Tech Solutions
Supporting production, traceability, logistics and market access.

Informal & Township Economies
Unlocking visibility, inclusion and economic participation for local enterprises.

Renewable Energy
Supporting community participation, supplier development and sustainable growth.

e-Tourism
Adventure, culture, eco and wellness experiences connected through digital participation.

### People → Markets → Services → Capital → Growth

The organisations that can connect participation with opportunity will help shape the next phase of economic development.
