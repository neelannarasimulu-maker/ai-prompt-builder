### Participation Creates Sustainable Revenue

The Thenga business model is designed around participation.

As more people, organisations and communities engage with the ecosystem, new opportunities emerge for transactions, services, marketplace activity and value-added participation.

### Revenue Streams

Platform Access – enabling ecosystem participation.

Transaction Revenue – generated through value exchange.

Marketplace Revenue – created through ecosystem activity.

Data Services – delivering insight and measurement.

Partner Services – supporting ecosystem participants.

Campaign Revenue – enabling targeted engagement.

### Growth Flywheel

More Participants → More Transactions → More Ecosystem Value → More Revenue Opportunities
