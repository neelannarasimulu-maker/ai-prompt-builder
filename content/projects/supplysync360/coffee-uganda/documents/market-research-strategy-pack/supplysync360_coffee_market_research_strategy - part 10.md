# Appendix A

# South African Coffee Market Statistics

## Market Characteristics

South Africa remains one of the largest coffee-consuming markets in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Key market characteristics include:

- Strong import dependency
- Growing specialty coffee segment
- Mature roasting industry
- Expanding café culture
- Growing premium coffee demand
- Increasing interest in traceability and sustainability

---

## Coffee Import Characteristics

South Africa imports substantially more green coffee than roasted coffee.

This demonstrates that value addition primarily occurs domestically through:

- Roasting
- Blending
- Packaging
- Distribution

This market structure supports the recommendation that SupplySync360 initially focus on green coffee imports rather than consumer-ready products.

---

## Major Coffee Origins

Common coffee origins serving the South African market include:

- Brazil
- Ethiopia
- Uganda
- Tanzania
- Colombia
- Guatemala
- Vietnam
- Rwanda

These origins collectively support commercial, specialty and premium coffee segments.
