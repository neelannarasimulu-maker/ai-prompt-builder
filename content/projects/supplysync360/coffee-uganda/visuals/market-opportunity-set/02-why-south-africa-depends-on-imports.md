## Intent

Explain the structural reasons South Africa depends on imported coffee.

## Visible Text

### Why South Africa Depends On Imported Coffee

Most coffee consumed in South Africa originates outside the country.

Local production is limited while roasting, blending and packaging capability is well developed.

South African coffee businesses prefer access to multiple international origins to support quality, consistency and blend flexibility.

Imported green coffee supports retail, hospitality, café, office and foodservice channels.

### Market Characteristics

Import Dependent Supply Chain

Growing Café Culture
