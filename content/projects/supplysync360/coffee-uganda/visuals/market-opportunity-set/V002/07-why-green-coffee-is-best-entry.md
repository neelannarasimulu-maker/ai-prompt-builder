## Intent

Clearly explain why green coffee is the best initial import option versus roasted, capsules, RTD or branded retail products.

## Visible Text

### Why Green Coffee Is The Best Entry Option

Green coffee is the dominant import category.

It is already the main product format used by South African roasters and coffee businesses.

Green coffee offers:

Largest addressable import volume

Lower brand-development burden

Local roasting flexibility

Multiple buyer segments

Lower retail-compliance complexity

No need to build a consumer brand first

Roasted coffee, capsules and ready-to-drink products may offer higher margins later, but they require stronger branding, packaging, retail access and channel investment.

### Strategic Conclusion

Start with green coffee to validate demand, then move into value-added formats once buyer relationships are proven.
