## Intent

Explain Ethiopia's position in South African imports and its role as the premium specialty origin.

## Visible Text

### Ethiopia: Premium Origin With Existing Demand

Ethiopia supplied approximately 1.39 million kg of non-decaffeinated green coffee to South Africa in 2024.

The import value was approximately US$8.30 million.

Ethiopia's implied South African import unit value was approximately US$5.98 per kg, materially higher than Uganda's US$4.01 per kg.

This pricing reflects Ethiopia's stronger premium and specialty positioning.

### Strategic Role

Ethiopia is best suited to specialty roasters, premium cafés, direct-trade programmes, limited releases and high-margin origin-led coffee products.

Ethiopia should complement Uganda rather than replace it.
