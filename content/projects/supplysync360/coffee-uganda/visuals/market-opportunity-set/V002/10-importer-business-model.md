## Intent

Explain how the opportunity becomes a viable business model rather than only a sourcing idea.

## Visible Text

### Importer-Distributor Business Model

The viable business is not just buying coffee.

It is coordinating supply, demand, quality, logistics and local availability.

Potential revenue layers:

Green coffee margin

Break-bulk distribution

Warehousing and fulfilment

Private-label partnerships

Premium specialty lots

Future technology services

### Business Logic

Many smaller roasters and coffee businesses cannot efficiently import directly from East Africa.

SupplySync360 can aggregate demand, import in controlled volumes, hold local stock and supply buyers in flexible quantities.

This creates value through access, reliability and coordination.
