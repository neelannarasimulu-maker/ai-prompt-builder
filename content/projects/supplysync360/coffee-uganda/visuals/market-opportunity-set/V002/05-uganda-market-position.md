## Intent

Explain Uganda's current position in South African green coffee imports and why it is commercially attractive.

## Visible Text

### Uganda: Existing Market Acceptance

Uganda supplied approximately 1.23 million kg of non-decaffeinated green coffee to South Africa in 2024.

This placed Uganda among South Africa's leading green coffee origin countries.

Uganda's 2024 green coffee import value into South Africa was approximately US$4.93 million.

Uganda's implied South African import unit value was approximately US$4.01 per kg based on WITS value and volume data.

### Why Uganda Matters

Uganda offers large Robusta production, growing Arabica supply, competitive pricing and commercial coffee scalability.

This makes Uganda well suited to commercial roasters, hospitality suppliers, corporate coffee programmes and espresso blend producers.
