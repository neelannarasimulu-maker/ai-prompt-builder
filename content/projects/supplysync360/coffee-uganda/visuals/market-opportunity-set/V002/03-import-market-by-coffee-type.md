## Intent

Quantify South Africa's coffee imports by type and show why green coffee dominates the opportunity.

## Visible Text

### Coffee Import Market By Type

South Africa's 2024 coffee imports show a clear preference for green coffee.

Import volumes:

Non-decaf green coffee:
22.7 million kg

Roasted non-decaf coffee:
1.4 million kg

Decaf green coffee:
397,000 kg

Roasted decaf coffee:
43,000 kg

The four-category import basket totalled approximately 24.6 million kg.

Non-decaffeinated green coffee alone represented about 92% of this volume.

### Strategic Implication

The market prefers to import green beans and perform roasting, blending, packaging and brand development locally.

Green coffee is the logical entry product.

Create a clean import mix visual with four coffee-type cards. Emphasise green coffee as the dominant import category and show local roasting as the value-add stage.
