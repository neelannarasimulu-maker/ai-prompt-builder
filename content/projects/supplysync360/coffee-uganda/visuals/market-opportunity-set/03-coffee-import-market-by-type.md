## Intent

Show the composition of South Africa's coffee imports.

## Visible Text

### Coffee Import Market By Type

Green coffee represents the overwhelming majority of coffee imported into South Africa.

Approximately 22.7 million kilograms of green coffee were imported during 2024 compared to approximately 1.4 million kilograms of roasted coffee.

This highlights the preference for local roasting, blending and packaging.

### Strategic Insight

Green coffee provides the largest addressable market opportunity.
