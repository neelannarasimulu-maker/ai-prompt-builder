## Intent

Explain why green coffee is the preferred market-entry strategy.

## Visible Text

### Why Green Coffee Is The Best Entry Strategy

South African buyers import significantly more green coffee than roasted coffee.

Local roasters prefer to control roasting, blending and packaging according to their own requirements.

Green coffee can support multiple customer segments simultaneously.

Green coffee also avoids many of the branding and retail-access challenges associated with finished products.

### Strategic Conclusion

Green coffee offers the most scalable and lowest-risk route into the South African market.
