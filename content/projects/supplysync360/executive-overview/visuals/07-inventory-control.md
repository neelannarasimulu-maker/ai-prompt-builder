## Intent

Show how SupplySync360 protects working capital and service performance through better inventory control.

## Visible Text

Inventory Control

Inventory represents cash, availability, service quality and operational risk.

SupplySync360 helps organisations understand what is available, what is ageing, what is moving and what requires action.

The platform supports inventory visibility across locations, categories, batches and operational workflows.

Teams can identify risk earlier and respond before it becomes a financial or service problem.

Better inventory control leads to stronger working capital management and improved operational reliability.
