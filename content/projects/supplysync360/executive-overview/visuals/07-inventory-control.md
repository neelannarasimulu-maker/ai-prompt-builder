## Intent

Show how SupplySync360 protects working capital and service performance through better inventory control.

## Visible Text

### Inventory Control

### Inventory represents cash, availability, service quality and operational risk.

SupplySync360 helps organisations understand what is available, what is ageing, what is moving and what requires action.

The platform supports inventory visibility across locations, categories, batches and operational workflows.

Teams can identify risk earlier and respond before it becomes a financial or service problem.

### Better inventory control protects working capital and service reliability.

Key control questions:
What do we have?
Where is it?
What is ageing?
What is moving?
What requires action?

### Inventory visibility becomes valuable when it triggers action.

## Image Brief

Create a premium inventory control visual showing stock movement, stock risk and operational visibility connected through a unified control layer.

The visual should emphasise value protection, cash tied in stock, ageing inventory, batch/location awareness and action-ready visibility.

Use a structured scene with warehouse, stockroom, retail or healthcare-inspired inventory elements, but keep the composition executive and uncluttered.

Show inventory flows moving through a central control layer with subtle risk markers and one restrained signal-yellow action cue.

No readable product labels, fake dashboards, product brands, generic stock icons or cluttered warehouse imagery.
