## Image Brief

Create a premium multi-industry visual showing different operational environments connected through a shared SupplySync360 control layer.

Represent pharmacies, healthcare, emergency medical services, retail, agriculture, warehousing, branch networks and fulfilment operations as abstract but recognisable environments.

The visual should show one platform pattern supporting many real-world contexts without making the slide feel like a generic icon grid.

Use spacious composition, hub-and-spoke or connected ecosystem structure, clear teal signal paths and restrained yellow emphasis for the shared control layer.

No readable text, industry logos, crowded scenes, fake dashboards or stock-style generic icons.
