## Intent

Explain the SupplySync360 operating model and demonstrate how fragmented signals become measurable action through a structured control loop.

## Visible Text

The SupplySync360 Control Loop

Every operational improvement begins with a signal.

A stock risk. A supplier delay. A demand change. A fulfilment exception.

SupplySync360 captures these signals and converts them into a structured operating process.

Signals are identified. Risks are prioritised. Actions are recommended. Ownership is assigned. Progress is tracked. Outcomes are measured.

The result is a repeatable control loop that improves visibility, accountability and execution across the organisation.

Visibility becomes valuable because it leads to action.
