## Intent

Present the core capabilities that enable SupplySync360 to coordinate suppliers, inventory, demand and execution across the organisation.

## Visible Text

Title: Core Capabilities
Section: Supplier Coordination
Body: Track supplier performance, order status, delivery exceptions, substitutions, delays and resolution activity through a single operational view.
Section: Inventory Visibility
Body: Monitor stock position, movement, batch risk, expiry exposure and location-level availability across the organisation.
Section: Order-to-Receive Control
Body: Provide visibility from request and approval through ordering, dispatch, receiving, reconciliation and closure.
Section: Expiry and Stock Risk Management
Body: Identify ageing inventory, slow-moving stock, excess holdings and at-risk inventory before value is lost.
Section: Forecasting and Replenishment Support
Body: Combine inventory, demand, supplier behaviour and operational signals to support more informed replenishment decisions.
Section: AI-Assisted Prioritisation
Body: Surface exceptions, risks and opportunities that require attention first, helping teams focus on what matters most.
Section: Workflow and User Management
Body: Create ownership, accountability, escalation paths and operational discipline across teams and processes.
Section: Governance and Auditability
Body: Provide approval controls, audit trails, exception history and evidence-based decision support.
Section: Dashboards, Alerts and Operating Evidence
Body: Deliver operational visibility, performance insights and measurable evidence of execution outcomes.
Action: Together these capabilities create one practical operating framework.

## Image Brief

Create an executive capability-map visual showing multiple operating modules arranged around a central SupplySync360 platform layer. The layout should use structured capability clusters rather than a large hero image, making the nine capabilities feel connected, balanced and boardroom-ready. Show supplier coordination, inventory, order-to-receive, risk management, forecasting, AI prioritisation, workflow, governance and operating evidence as linked cards or modular tiles. Use clean hierarchy, consistent card styling, subtle teal signal paths and restrained yellow priority indicators to show the capabilities working together. No generic app icons, crowded dashboards, fake metrics, unreadable UI text or overly technical interface screens.
