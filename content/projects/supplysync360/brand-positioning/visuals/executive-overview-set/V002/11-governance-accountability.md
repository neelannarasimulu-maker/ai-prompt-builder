## Image Brief

Create an executive governance visual showing actions, ownership, escalation and closure connected through a traceable operating framework. Use layered evidence cards, workflow paths, ownership nodes and executive oversight cues to communicate control and accountability without feeling bureaucratic. The composition should show a clean chain from issue identification to owner assignment, escalation, approval, resolution and closure evidence. Use teal/deep-teal signal paths, soft evidence panels and one restrained yellow overdue or escalation cue. No readable documents, legal text, fake compliance dashboards, generic shield/check icons or cluttered administrative imagery.
