## LinkedIn Post Text
Emergency and field-service logistics are often judged at the moment of dispatch.

But many failures begin earlier.

The stock was not assigned. The pack was incomplete. The batch was not visible. The handover was unclear. The proof was missing. The receiving team had no reliable evidence. The gap was discovered only when time was already expensive.

Readiness is a supply-chain discipline.

It requires item visibility, location status, owner assignment, movement tracking, handover evidence and closure. For high-pressure operations, that evidence must be practical, fast and traceable.

SupplySync360 supports this kind of operating control: inventory assignment, transit status, QR-linked batches, proof-of-receipt and exception workflows.

In time-critical logistics, control starts before movement.
