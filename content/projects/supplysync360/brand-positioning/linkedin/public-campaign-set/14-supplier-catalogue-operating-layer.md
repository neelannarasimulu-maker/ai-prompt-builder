## LinkedIn Post Text

A catalogue that only lists products does not solve a supply-chain problem.

In real operations, the value comes when product data is connected to the decisions teams must make every day.

Can this item be ordered?
Which supplier can fulfil it?
Is it visible to the right linked business?
Is it part of an assembly, kit or bundle?
Is the stock position changing?
Does this product need replenishment, transfer, substitution or escalation?

That is where a catalogue becomes an operating layer.

SupplySync360 supports product maintenance, supplier products, product assemblies and controlled publishing for linked businesses. The aim is to turn catalogue data into a practical coordination tool for procurement, branch operations and replenishment.

The stronger catalogue is not a list. It is a decision surface.
