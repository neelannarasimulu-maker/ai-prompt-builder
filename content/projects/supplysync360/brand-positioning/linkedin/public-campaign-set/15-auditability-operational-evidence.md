## LinkedIn Post Text
Auditability is not only for compliance teams.

It is how operations prove what happened.

When stock moves, orders change, returns are processed, proof is uploaded, an exception is approved or a workflow is closed, management needs a reliable trail.

Without that evidence, teams lose time in confusion:

Who changed the quantity?
Was the stock actually received?
Was the transfer approved?
Why did the return happen?
Who owned the exception?
What changed after closure?

SupplySync360 is positioned to turn operating activity into usable evidence: audit records, version visibility, user activity context, movement history, receipt proof and exception closure across operational modules.

The audit trail is not the paperwork after the work.
It is part of the control system.
