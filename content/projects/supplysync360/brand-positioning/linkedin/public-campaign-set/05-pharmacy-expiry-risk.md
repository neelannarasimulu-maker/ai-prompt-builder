## LinkedIn Post Text
Pharmacy availability is now a visibility challenge

In pharmacy, stock on the shelf is only one part of availability. Batch, expiry, supplier timing and branch imbalance matter just as much.

Pharmacy teams know the pressure: patients expect availability, suppliers may be constrained, and stock that is useful in one branch can expire quietly in another.

The operational risk is not only expiry. It is delayed visibility: seeing the batch, location and demand signal too late to transfer, use first or escalate.

The stronger model treats expiry as an active workflow, not a month-end discovery.

SupplySync360 supports item, batch, expiry, location and linked-business visibility so pharmacy teams can act before stock becomes either unavailable or waste.
