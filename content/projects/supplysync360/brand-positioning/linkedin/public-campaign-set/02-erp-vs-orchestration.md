## LinkedIn Post Text
ERP is not enough when the supply chain is moving

Your ERP can record the transaction. It cannot always coordinate the decision when a truck is delayed, a branch is short and a supplier has changed the promise.

In a stable environment, recording the transaction may feel close to control. In South Africa, the operating environment is too variable for that assumption.

A purchase order can be correct while the supplier is late. Stock can exist in the network while the wrong branch is short. A delivery can be dispatched while receiving evidence is still missing.

The gap is not ERP versus orchestration. It is ERP for trusted records, plus an operating layer that coordinates what teams must do next.

SupplySync360 sits in that execution gap: inventory, orders, suppliers, fulfilment, users, permissions and audit in one operational control layer.
