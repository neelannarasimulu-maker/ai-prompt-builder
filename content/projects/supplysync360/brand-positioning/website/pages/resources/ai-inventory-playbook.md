# Inventory Intelligence Playbook

Reduce stock risk and working capital pressure while protecting service levels with clear replenishment policies.

## In Simple Language

This playbook helps teams improve stock decisions using better forecasting and clear replenishment rules.

## Guide Overview

Inventory optimisation works best when demand variability, supplier reliability, and service targets are managed together.

Teams improve outcomes faster when they can compare scenarios, test assumptions, and apply practical guardrails.

The strongest results come from connecting inventory decisions with procurement timing and distribution realities.

## Assumptions

- Forecasting inputs are refreshed regularly.
- Service targets are agreed by product segment.
- Supplier lead-time variability is monitored.
- Replenishment decisions are reviewed through defined governance.

## How SupplySync360 Delivers These Outcomes

1. Combines forecasting, supplier performance, and replenishment controls in a single planning loop.
2. Supports scenario comparisons so teams can test service and inventory trade-offs.
3. Applies policy guardrails to protect availability while limiting overstock risk.
4. Links recommendations directly to operational execution and exception management.
