# Retail

Run a dual marketing model that serves modern trade key accounts and independent neighborhood stores with one coordinated supply engine.

## Network Challenge

Retail teams must protect on-shelf availability for national chains while also supporting high-frequency, low-volume replenishment for fragmented local outlets.

## Orchestration Approach

Use one planning backbone with channel-specific assortment, pricing cadence, and service rules so enterprise account programmes and SME trade activation execute in parallel.

## Expected Operational Outcomes

1. Higher shelf availability across modern and traditional trade
2. Faster promo-to-replenishment response by channel
3. Stronger OTIF performance with clearer store-level accountability
