# Agriculture

Run a dual agriculture marketing strategy that serves large institutional buyers and fragmented agro-dealer networks from one system.

## Network Challenge

Agriculture supply chains must balance seasonal bulk commitments to enterprise buyers with frequent, localized replenishment to rural SME channels.

## Orchestration Approach

Plan seasonally at aggregate level, execute tactically at micro-market level, and apply channel-specific policies for quality, lot traceability, and replenishment frequency.

## Expected Operational Outcomes

1. Better seasonal service reliability for major contracts
2. Improved rural channel availability with lower emergency dispatches
3. Stronger lot-control and traceability across distributed networks
