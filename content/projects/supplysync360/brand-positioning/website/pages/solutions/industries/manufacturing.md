# Manufacturing

Support a dual marketing manufacturing strategy by synchronising OEM/key-account fulfilment and aftermarket channel replenishment.

## Network Challenge

Manufacturers must protect contract commitments for large buyers while rapidly supplying diverse aftermarket partners with different order patterns and SLAs.

## Orchestration Approach

Create a segmented control tower that separates make-to-contract and make-to-market signals, then orchestrates supplier, inventory, and logistics decisions by channel priority.

## Expected Operational Outcomes

1. Reduced service risk for strategic enterprise accounts
2. More reliable availability across dealer and parts networks
3. Clearer cross-channel allocation and escalation governance
