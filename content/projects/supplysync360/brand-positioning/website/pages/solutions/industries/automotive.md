# Automotive

Enable a dual automotive go-to-market motion across OEM production demand and dealer/aftermarket service demand.

## Network Challenge

Automotive networks face conflicting priorities between just-in-time plant supply and unpredictable dealer or service-part consumption.

## Orchestration Approach

Segment demand streams by production-critical and market-responsive flows, then apply differentiated sourcing, stocking, and transport controls for each channel.

## Expected Operational Outcomes

1. Improved continuity for production-critical components
2. Faster service-part fulfilment to dealer networks
3. Higher visibility of tiered supplier and logistics risk
